Date & Nut Energy Bar

Boost your energy with these delicious date and nut bars, perfect for a quick snack or post-workout fuel and packed with natural sweetness and wholesome ingredients.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 1 hour 15 minutes
  • Preparation: 15 minutes
  • Cooking: 1 hour

Ingredients

  • 1 cup raw nuts (mixed, I like to split between 2 varieties)
  • 1 cup medjool dates
  • 1 cut dried fruit of choice
  • 2 tbsp hemp hearts
  • 2 tbsp flax seeds
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1/3 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Directions

  1. Add the nuts to the food processor and pulse until minced
  2. Add the seeds, cinnamon, and salt - pulse to blend
  3. Add the dried fruit
  4. Run in high speed until the mixture comes together in a ball. Scraping down the sides as needed
  5. Spread evenly into a pan
  6. Chill for at least 1 hour before slicing into rectangles
